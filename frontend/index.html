About AgriPredict

Empowering Karnataka farmers with AI-driven commodity price intelligence since 2026.

🎯

Our Mission

To bridge the information gap between farmers and markets by providing real-time commodity prices and AI-powered forecasts, helping every farmer in Karnataka make smarter, more profitable decisions.

👁

Our Vision

A future where every farmer has access to the same market intelligence as large agri-businesses — transparent, data-driven, and free.
